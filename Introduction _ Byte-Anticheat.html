Byte Anticheat (ByteAC)

ByteAC is an anticheat resource for FiveM. It runs locally on your server.

You can also manage ByteAC from a web panel for pairing, settings, and staff access.

What you get

  • Client-side detections plus server-side validation.

  • A JSON-based ban system.

  • Discord logging via webhooks.

  • A web panel for pairing and live configuration.

How it works (high level)

  1. The client reports relevant signals and state.

  2. The server validates and correlates events.

  3. On detection, ByteAC applies an action (for example, kick).

  4. The event is sent to Discord.

  5. For bans, an entry is written to JSON and enforced on connect.

ByteAC is designed to be drop-in. Pairing and most tuning happens in the panel.

An anticheat does not replace moderation. Keep a review workflow for staff.
